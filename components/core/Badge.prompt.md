Compact pill label for status, category, or count — risk tones mirror the traffic-light system.

```jsx
<Badge tone="safe" dot>On track</Badge>
<Badge tone="caution">Check this</Badge>
<Badge tone="brand">Dermatology</Badge>
```

Tones: `neutral`, `brand`, `accent`, `safe`, `caution`, `risk`. Props: `dot` (leading status dot), `iconLeft`.
